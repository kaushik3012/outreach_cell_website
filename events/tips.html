Tips from the Top Sessions

Organized by Outreach Cell, Tips from the Top is one of the awaited events of the year, where students get a chance to have one-on-one interactions with alumni who are at the top of their respective fields and get further insights into various career paths. These sessions are organized twice every year, once every semester.
